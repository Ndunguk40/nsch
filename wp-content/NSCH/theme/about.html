WHO WE ARE

/Nyambene Sub-County Hospital is a level-4 Hospital located in Maua Town, Igembe-South Sub-County leading in medical expertise and service provision.We take pride in our highly qualified professionals who deliver our mission each day by translating their knowledge and expertise to internationally compliant practices in healthcare provision.Our dedicated staff provides professional care within a friendly and comfortable environment, ensuring a pleasant and anxiety-free time in hospital for our patients and their families

Mission

To promote and participate in the provision of intergrated and high quality curative and rehabilitative services to meru county and all Kenyans.

Vision

To have an efficient and cost effective medical care system for a healthy nation

Payments can be done through cash or insurance cover such as NHIF

What is the outpatient process at Nyambene Sub-County Hospital?

Upon arrival, you are required to proceed to the registration desk where personal information is taken and financial clearance is done. You are then directed by the staff to the next stage.
